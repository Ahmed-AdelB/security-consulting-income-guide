    PayScale Security Salary Research 2025: Ultra Deep Analysis¶

    2,000+ Data Points Across 8 Security Roles¶

    Research Date: December 23, 2025 Primary Source: PayScale + Multi-source validation (Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, Salary.com, Indeed) Geographic Focus: United States (with international comparisons)

    EXECUTIVE SUMMARY¶

    This comprehensive research analyzes salary data for 8 critical cybersecurity roles, drawing from PayScale and multiple validated sources. Key findings:

    • Average salary range: $97,684 (Application Security Engineer) to $182,175 (CISO)
    • Typical salary spread: 10th to 90th percentile ranges show 2-3x variation within roles
    • Experience premium: 50-100% salary increase from entry-level to 5+ years experience
    • Location premium: Top tech hubs (San Francisco, New York, Seattle) offer 20-30% higher compensation
    • Industry premium: Information Technology and Finance sectors pay 15-40% above average
    • Contractor premium: Freelance/contract rates command 30-70% premiums over full-time salaries
    • Market shortage: 3.5M unfilled cybersecurity positions globally driving upward salary pressure
