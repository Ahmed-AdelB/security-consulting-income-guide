## RED FLAGS - AVOID THESE

**Platform Red Flags:**

- Programs with <10 resolved reports
- No public payouts visible
- Threatening legal language
- Unresponsive (30+ days no triage)
- Change scope after report
- Only offer "swag" or "recognition"

**HackerOne Specific:**

- Payment delays (weeks to months)
- Mediation ineffective (criticized)
- Platform sides with customers
- Some programs "ghost" researchers
