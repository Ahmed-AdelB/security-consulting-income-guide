# Comprehensive Security Salary Research Report 2025
**Sources:** Glassdoor, PayScale, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Salary.com, and Industry Reports.

## Executive Summary
This report aggregates compensation data for **Security Consultants**, **Penetration Testers**, and **Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs)** for the year 2025. The data reflects a robust market where specialization, leadership, and security clearances significantly drive earning potential. While entry-level roles remain competitive, senior and executive roles—particularly in major tech hubs—command substantial premiums.

| **IANS Research**| N/A | $160k – $3.2M | Highlights the disparity. Top 1% start at **$3.2M** total comp. |

### Insights
*   **The "CISO Gap":** There is a massive divide between CISOs at small/medium businesses (~$150k) and Fortune 500 enterprises ($400k - $1M+).
*   **Equity is King:** For top-tier CISO roles, **stock options and equity** often double the base salary, bringing total compensation packages to over $700k annually.
*   **Startup Market:** Early-stage startup CISOs typically see **$160k - $210k** + Equity.

## 4. Key 2025 Trends
1.  **Clearance Premium:** A **Top Secret** clearance continues to add a premium of roughly **$20,000 - $30,000** to base salaries, especially in the DC/NoVA area.
2.  **Specialist vs. Generalist:** "Full-stack" security generalist pay is leveling off. **Deep specialists** (AI Security, Cloud Architecture, Threat Hunting) are seeing the fastest salary growth.
3.  **Location Independence:** While remote work remains common, "tier 1" cities (SF, NYC, Seattle) still dictate the highest bands. Remote roles often adjust down by 10-15% depending on the candidate's residence.
4.  **Contracting Rates:**
    *   **Security Consultant:** $100 - $250 / hour
    *   **Senior Pentester:** $150 - $300 / hour
    *   **Virtual CISO (vCISO):** $200 - $500 / hour

**Disclaimer:** *Figures presented are averages and estimates based on available data for 2025. Actual compensation depends heavily on negotiation, specific technical skills, and company equity structures.*
