## Startup Security Stage Map

### Pre‑Seed / Pre‑Revenue
- Engagement archetype: Lightweight advisor (1–2 hrs/month) with scope‑limited guidance.
- Equity bands for lightweight advisors are highest in pre‑seed stages (0.25%–0.75%).
- Early focus is on asset inventory and top‑10 risk register creation.
- Deliverable emphasis is on risk assessment and roadmap creation, not deep controls.
- Fractional/vCISO hourly anchors start at $150–$250/hr from local benchmarks.
- Fixed‑fee discovery work commonly lands in the $7.5k–$15k range for early scoping.
- SOC 2 readiness is usually scoped but not executed until an enterprise deal appears.
- Advisory scope centers on security posture narrative for investor updates.
- Compliance hook remains a sales‑trigger, not a compliance mandate.
- Early engagements should include risk ownership clarity and escalation paths.

### Seed Stage
- Enterprise customers can demand SOC 2 or ISO 27001 even at seed stage.
- Startups adopt fractional leadership to avoid full‑time CISO cost.
- Typical seed engagement: discovery + risk assessment at $7.5k–$15k.
- Typical seed retainer: $3k–$7.5k/month for 8–15 hrs/month.
- SOC 2 readiness budget for early‑stage SaaS: $15k–$50k (consulting only).
- SOC 2 readiness consultant projects frequently price at $5k–$20k.
- Cloud security assessments often price at $5k–$25k.
- Compliance automation tooling (Vanta/Drata) is a common early investment.
- Policy suite initialization becomes a priority (security, incident, change, vendor).
- Audit readiness projects are positioned as sales acceleration work.

### Series A
- SOC 2 readiness is a primary enterprise sales blocker and investor expectation.
- Series A security pitches emphasize “SOC 2 readiness in 6 months.”
- Series A retainer example from local data: $10k/month per client.
- SOC 2 readiness standard engagements range $15k–$25k and premium $25k–$85k+.
- Seed/Series A board compensation is cash‑light and equity‑heavy.
- Seed/Series A board cash bands are $0–$25k with 0.25%–1.00% equity.
- Series A advisor equity bands: 0.10%–0.30% (light), 0.20%–0.50% (active), 0.35%–1.0% (fractional).
- Series A security roadmap usually includes policy suite, evidence plan, and control owners.
- Series A projects benefit from fixed‑fee readiness packages (e.g., $15k Type I readiness in 4 weeks).
- Risk register ownership and board reporting cadence begin to formalize.

### Series B
- Series B funding is a compliance trigger in the vCISO guide.
- Series B retainer example from local data: $15k/month per client.
- Series B engagements often add executive dashboards and board reporting packs.
- SOC 2 Type II evidence periods create ongoing compliance ops retainers.
- Continuous compliance retainer ranges sit at $5k–$20k/month.
- Premium retainers in vCISO analysis range $7k–$12k/month for 40+ hours.
- Program builds stretch to 8–24+ weeks when control implementations expand.
- Larger vendor ecosystems increase vendor risk policy scope.
- Incident playbooks and tabletop exercises become standard deliverables.
- Coverage goals expand toward 90% scanning and evidence automation.

### Series C+ / Pre‑IPO
- Late‑stage CISO total comp targets $350k–$500k+ with equity weight.
- Security leadership roles become full‑time with board‑level accountability.
- Governance routines include quarterly board updates and KPI reporting.
- Audit and certification work is multi‑framework with SOC 2 + ISO mapping.
- Advisory equity ranges compress toward 0.02%–0.10% as dilution grows.
- Compliance operations shift into continuous control monitoring.
- Security spend prioritizes audit‑ready evidence automation and GRC tooling.
- Vendor risk and third‑party assessments scale with enterprise contracts.
- Additional premium rates apply in regulated sectors (fintech, healthcare).
- M&A and investor due diligence become ongoing requirements.

## SOC 2 Readiness Foundations

### Demand Drivers (Local Synthesis)
- Enterprise procurement requirements drive SOC 2 and ISO 27001 requests.
- Regulatory exposure and enforcement amplify compliance urgency.
- Investor and M&A due diligence requires security posture narratives.
- Competitive differentiation for SaaS vendors favors audit‑ready posture.
- Cyber insurance underwriting expects documented minimum controls.

### Engagement Models and Typical Timelines
- Readiness assessment (4–8 weeks) for fast gap discovery.
- Program build (8–24+ weeks) for policies, controls, and evidence.
- Audit/certification support (4–12 weeks) for external audit prep.
- Continuous compliance retainer for ongoing evidence collection.
- vCISO / privacy officer retainer for strategic governance.

- Security policy documentation.
- Privacy policy documentation.
- Vendor management policy documentation.
- Change management policy documentation.
- Incident response policy documentation.
- Business continuity and disaster recovery policy documentation.
- Evidence matrix with collection cadence.
- Gap remediation plan with timelines and owners.
- Audit/assessment support and evidence packaging.

### SOC 2 Consulting Focus Areas
- SOC 2 scoping and boundary definition.
- Control selection mapped to Trust Services Criteria.
- Policy and procedure development for key domains.
- Evidence strategy design and collection automation.
- Gap remediation (access controls, logging, change management).
- Control testing readiness and evidence packaging.
- Auditor coordination and report review.

### SOC 2 Cost Drivers (GRC 10K)
- Type II period length and evidence complexity.
- Number of in‑scope systems and vendors.
- Security tooling maturity (SSO, SIEM, ticketing).
- Volume of manual evidence collection.
- Complexity of system description and subservice carve‑outs.
