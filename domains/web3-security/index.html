    Platform Deep Dives & Top Earner Data¶

    1) Immunefi (Bug Bounty Jackpot Model)¶

    Platform Snapshot

    • Launch: Dec 2020.
    • Researchers: 45,000+.
    • Projects protected: 310+.
    • Paid reports: 3,000+.
    • Assets secured: $190B+.
    • Total payouts: $100M+ (range $100.21M–$116M in local sources).

    Earnings Mechanics

    • Critical rewards: often 10% of funds at risk, with $1M–$10M caps.
    • Median payout: ~$2,000; average payout: ~$52,800.
    • Typical critical bug: $50k–$250k; low severity: $25–$5,000.

    • Elite earners are highly concentrated: top 10 combined earnings ~$42M.

    2) Code4rena (Competitive Audit Contests)¶

    Platform Snapshot

    • Launch: 2021.
    • Registered wardens: 10k+ (legacy) to 16,600+ (2025 dataset).
    • 2023 contest days: 966; 2023 first-time prize earners: 927.
    • Business model: 100% of contest funds to auditors/judges (as of 2024).

    Contest Economics

    • Typical prize pool: $30k–$100k (dataset often cites ~$50k).
    • Large pools: $100k–$500k+ (Monad $500k; Size $200k; Kakarot $170k).
    • Highest single contest payout (2023): $71,500.

    3) Sherlock (Underwritten Audit Contests)¶

    Platform Snapshot

    • Launch: 2022.
    • Registered Watsons: 10,000+; contests hosted: 175+.
    • Total payouts: $14.8M+.
    • Insurance coverage: up to $2M for missed vulnerabilities.

    Earnings Structure

    • Lead Senior Watson (LSW): ~$12.5k–$20k per audit week + contest pot share.
    • Senior Watson (Top 10%): ~$10k+ per audit week.
    • Contest payouts stack on top of fixed stipends.

    Earnings Distribution & Competition Signals¶

    • Immunefi has 45k+ researchers; only ~3% have found a critical mainnet bug.
    • Code4rena has 10k–16.6k wardens; only ~13% earned prizes in 2023.
    • Sherlock’s Watson cohort is 10k+, but with a higher bar for entry and ranking.
    • Spearbit access is invitation-based and typically requires elite contest or fellowship performance.

    Key Trends (2024-2025)¶

    1. Super-Contest Era: $2M Sherlock pools and $500k+ Code4rena pools are consolidating security budgets into fewer, larger events.
    2. Specialization Advantage: Top earners focus on niche ecosystems (ZK-rollups, bridges, complex DeFi).
    3. Smart Contract Dominance: 77.5% of Immunefi payouts are for smart contract bugs.
    4. Formalized Elite Tiers: Immunefi “All Stars,” Sherlock senior Watsons, and Spearbit tiers widen the gap between casual and elite earners.

    Practical Implications for Income Planning¶

    • Maximum upside: Immunefi offers the only documented path to $3M+ individual earnings, but relies on rare critical finds.
    • High-variance but scalable: Code4rena contests support $500k–$1M annual for top wardens with consistent placements.
    • Structured elite path: Sherlock’s LSW/Senior Watson model provides more predictable high pay for experienced auditors.
    • Stability-first: Spearbit’s salary model delivers guaranteed high six-figure to seven-figure income.
