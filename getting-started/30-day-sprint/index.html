    CODEX 30-DAY SPRINT GUIDE: DAILY ACTION PLAN FOR FIRST $10K¶

    Target: $10,000 in booked revenue (billed or retainer) by Day 30. Strategy: Aggressive platform activation -> Rapid rate calibration -> Retainer lock-in.

    üõë PRE-SPRINT CHECKLIST (Day 0)¶

    • Resume/CV Updated: Tailored for "Cybersecurity Consultant/Advisor" (not just employee).
    • 1-Page Bio: Professional PDF bio highlighting "15+ years," "CISO-level," and specific niches (Cloud, GRC, etc.).
    • Headshot: Professional quality (no selfies).
    • Calendar Blocked: 60 mins/day allocated strictly for this sprint (e.g., 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM).
    • Payment Method: Wise/PayPal/Stripe account ready for global payments.

    üóìÔ∏è WEEK 1: THE FOUNDATION (PROFILE & TIER 1 APPS)¶

    Goal: Submit perfect profiles to the top 10 highest-paying platforms. Theme: "Speed & Quality"

    Day 1: The Big Three (Expert Networks)¶

    • Action: Apply to GLG (Gerson Lehrman Group). Note: Set rate to $500/hr.
    • Action: Apply to AlphaSights. Note: Emphasize C-level decision-making experience.
    • Action: Apply to Guidepoint.
    • Task: Create a standard "Outreach Template" for recruiters (2-3 sentences: "I am a CISO specializing in [Niche]. Open to consults.")

    Day 2: Niche Dominance¶

    • Action: Apply to IANS Research (Cybersecurity specific - High Priority).
    • Action: Apply to Coleman Research.
    • Action: Apply to Third Bridge.
    • Task: LinkedIn update: Turn "Open to Work" setting to "Providing Services" (Consulting, Advisory).

    Day 3: The vCISO Pivot¶

    • Action: Create profile on SideChannel.
    • Action: Register on The vCISO Directory.
    • Action: Apply to Pivot Point Security (vCISO partner program).
    • Task: Draft a generic "vCISO One-Pager" service sheet (e.g., "Fractional CISO Services: Risk, Compliance, Strategy").

    Day 4: Expert Witness Setup (The "Whale" Hunter)¶

    • Action: Register on Round Table Group (Expert Witness).
    • Action: Register on Expert Institute.
    • Action: Register on IMS Legal Strategies.
    • Task: Add "Expert Witness" to your LinkedIn headline.

    Day 5: The "Quick Cash" Layer (Bug Bounty/Web3)¶

    • Action: Register on HackerOne & Bugcrowd.
    • Action: Register on Synack (Start the vetting process).
    • Action: Register on Code4rena (Web3 Audits).
    • Task: Spend 30 mins scanning active programs to identify 1 viable target.

    Day 6: Catch-Up & Polish¶

    • Action: Review all Day 1-5 applications. Check for "Pending Info" flags.
    • Action: Apply to Maven & Mosaic Research.
    • Task: Post on LinkedIn: "Excited to share I'm opening up limited slots for advisory/expert network calls regarding [Your Niche]."

    Day 7: Weekly Review¶

    • Metric: # of Applications Submitted (Target: 15+).
    • Metric: # of Profiles Approved (Target: 3+).
    • Plan: Adjust Day 8-14 targets based on approval speed.

    üóìÔ∏è WEEK 2: ACTIVATION & FIRST WINS¶

    Goal: Secure first screening calls & book first 1-2 consults. Theme: "Responsiveness"

    Day 8: Aggressive Follow-Up¶

    • Action: Email/Msg support for any pending Day 1-3 applications.
    • Action: Apply to Tegus & Dialectica.
    • Routine: Check ALL inboxes (Spam too) for recruiter invites. Respond < 30 mins.

    Day 9: The "Warm" Market¶

    • Action: Message 5 former colleagues/peers: "I'm doing independent consulting now. If you know any PE/VC firms needing due diligence, let me know."
    • Action: Apply to NewtonX (B2B specific).

    Day 10: Content for Credibility¶

    • Action: Post a "Case Study" snippet on LinkedIn (e.g., "How I solved a [Problem] for a Client").
    • Action: Apply to CleverX & ProSapient.
    • Routine: Login to GLG/AlphaSights to refresh "Last Active" timestamp.

    Day 11: Expansion (Tier 2)¶

    • Action: Apply to Atheneum.
    • Action: Apply to Inex One.
    • Task: Refine rate strategy. If rejected for rate, lower slightly ($400) just to get the first rating, then raise immediately.

    Day 12: Expert Witness Deep Dive¶

    • Action: Register on SEAK (Directory).
    • Action: Register on JurisPro.
    • Task: Search for 3 law firms specializing in "Data Breach Litigation" and find their partners on LinkedIn.

    Day 13: Web3/Bounty Weekend Prep¶

    • Action: Register on Sherlock (Web3).
    • Action: Register on Immunefi.
    • Task: Select ONE contest or program to work on for 4 hours this weekend.

    Day 14: Weekly Review & Rate Check¶

    • Metric: # of Screening Calls Booked.
    • Metric: # of Consults Completed.
    • Adjustment: If 0 calls, rewrite profile bio to be more "keyword stuffed" (e.g., specific vendor names, regulations).

    üóìÔ∏è WEEK 3: DEEPENING THE FUNNEL (RETAINERS)¶

    Goal: Pitch a $3k-$5k/mo retainer (vCISO) or secure a major Expert Witness case. Theme: "Upsell"

    Day 15: The vCISO Pitch¶

    • Action: Apply to RealCISO.
    • Action: Reach out to 5 MSPs (Managed Service Providers) in your area: "Do you need a vCISO partner for your clients?"
    • Routine: Check Expert Network portals.

    Day 16: Specialized Networks¶

    • Action: Apply to DeepBench (now Bridger).
    • Action: Apply to Techspert (AI focus).
    • Task: Follow up with MSPs from Day 15.

    Day 17: Speaking/Training Income¶

    • Action: Register on SANS (Instructor application) or Udemy (Instructor).
    • Task: Outline a 1-hour "Corporate Security Workshop" you could sell for $2k-$5k.

    Day 18: LinkedIn Outbound¶

    • Action: Connection request 10 Investment Associates at PE firms (Potential Diligence clients).
    • Note: "Hi [Name], I specialize in cybersecurity due diligence. If you need a technical look at a target, I'm available."

    Day 19: Proposal Day¶

    • Task: Draft a "vCISO Retainer Proposal" template ($5k/mo tier, $8k/mo tier).
    • Action: Apply to Catalant (High-end freelance).

    Day 20: Hunt for "Quick Wins"¶

    • Action: Check Upwork/Toptal (if registered) for high-ticket fixed-price gigs ($2k+ only).
    • Action: Check HackerOne for "easy" low-hanging fruit to build rep.

    Day 21: Weekly Review & Pipeline¶

    • Metric: $ Pipeline Value (Proposals out + Booked calls).
    • Goal: Should have $5k+ in pipeline by now.

    üóìÔ∏è WEEK 4: CLOSING & OPTIMIZATION¶

    Goal: Close the gap to $10k. Negotiate active deals. Theme: "Closing"

    Day 22: Negotiation Focus¶

    • Strategy: For any expert network request >$500, ask: "My standard rate is $X, can we meet there?"
    • Action: Follow up on all MSP/Law firm outreach.

    Day 23: The "Ghost" Revival¶

    • Action: Re-ping any recruiters who reached out but went silent. "Just checking in, still available for this."

    Day 24: Asset Creation¶

    • Task: Create a "One-Pager" Expert Witness CV (focus on credentials/experience, not job history).
    • Action: Submit to 3 more directories.

    Day 25: Web3/Bounty Push¶

    • Task: Submit findings from the weekend work (Day 13). Even a small payout adds momentum.

    Day 26: Referral Loop¶

    • Action: Ask a happy client (or peer): "Who else do you know that needs help with [Problem]?"

    Day 27: Rate Review¶

    • Task: If fully booked, raise rates on profile by 20% for NEW inquiries.

    Day 28: Administrative Catch-up¶

    • Task: Invoice any completed work immediately.
    • Task: Ensure tax forms (W-9/W-8BEN) are submitted to all platforms.

    Day 29: The "Next Month" Setup¶

    • Task: Plan Week 1 of Month 2. Focus on the top 20% of platforms that yielded results.

    Day 30: FINAL SPRINT REVIEW¶

    • Metric: Total Revenue Booked + Billed.
    • Metric: Average Hourly Rate Achieved.
    • Decision: Cut the bottom 50% of platforms that gave 0 leads. Double down on the winners.

    üß† DAILY NON-NEGOTIABLES (THE "MORNING 30")¶

    1. Check Email/Portals (15 mins): Respond to EVERYTHING. Speed = Revenue.
    2. LinkedIn Engagement (10 mins): Comment on 3 relevant posts (PE/VC, Legal, Cyber leaders).
    3. Application/Outreach (5 mins): Apply to 1 new platform or send 1 outreach message.

    üí∞ RATE GUIDANCE (STARTING)¶

    • Expert Networks: $300 - $500/hr (Move to $600+ after 3 calls).
    • Expert Witness: $400 - $600/hr (Move to $750+ after 1 case).
    • vCISO: $200 - $300/hr (or $3k - $8k/mo retainer).
    • Pentest/Audit: $150 - $250/hr.
