# Platform Deep Dives

Comprehensive guides for every platform where cybersecurity professionals can earn consulting income.

## Platform Categories

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

- :material-account-tie:{ .lg .middle } **Expert Networks**

  ***

  Connect with investors and executives for high-paying consultation calls.
  - GLG, AlphaSights, Guidepoint
  - $500-$2,000/hour rates
  - Flexible, on-demand work

  [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Expert Networks](expert-networks/)

- :material-bug:{ .lg .middle } **Bug Bounty Platforms**

  ***

  Find vulnerabilities and get paid by major companies.
  - HackerOne, Bugcrowd, Synack
  - $100-$50,000+ per bug
  - Build reputation and portfolio

  [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Bug Bounty](bug-bounty/)

- :material-shield-lock:{ .lg .middle } **vCISO Platforms**

  ***

  Provide fractional CISO services to multiple clients.
  - SideChannel, FRSecure, RealCISO
  - $5,000-$15,000/month retainers
  - Recurring revenue model

  [:octicons-arrow-right-24: vCISO](vciso/)

- :material-gavel:{ .lg .middle } **Expert Witness**

  ***

  Provide litigation support and testimony for legal cases.
  - SEAK, Expert Institute, Round Table
  - $300-$1,000+/hour rates
  - High-stakes, high-pay work

  [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Expert Witness](expert-witness/)

- :material-ethereum:{ .lg .middle } **Web3 Security Audits**

  ***

  Audit smart contracts and blockchain protocols.
  - Code4rena, Immunefi, Spearbit
  - $30,000-$500,000+ per contest
  - Cutting-edge technology

  [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Web3 Audit](web3-audit/)

| Platform Type   | Rate Range         | Time Commitment | Difficulty | Best For               |
| --------------- | ------------------ | --------------- | ---------- | ---------------------- |
| Expert Networks | $500-$2,000/hr     | 1-2 hrs/call    | Low        | All levels             |
| Bug Bounty      | $100-$50K/bug      | Flexible        | High       | Technical hunters      |
| vCISO           | $5K-$15K/mo        | 10-40 hrs/mo    | Medium     | Senior security        |
| Expert Witness  | $300-$1,000/hr     | Variable        | Medium     | Domain experts         |
| Web3 Audit      | $30K-$500K/contest | 1-4 weeks       | Very High  | Smart contract experts |
