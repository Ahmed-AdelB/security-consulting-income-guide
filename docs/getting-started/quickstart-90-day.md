# 90-Day Security Consulting Quickstart Guide

**From $0 to Your First $5K Month in 90 Days**

This guide provides a concrete, week-by-week action plan to start earning income as a security consultant.

---

## Before You Begin

### Prerequisites

- **5+ years** cybersecurity experience (DFIR, GRC, pentest, cloud security, etc.)
- **At least 1** industry certification (CISSP, OSCP, GIAC, etc.)
- **LinkedIn profile** with complete work history
- **2-4 hours/week** available for side consulting (while employed)

### Target by Day 90

- **$3,000-$8,000** in monthly consulting income
- **3-5** active platform profiles
- **$300-$500/hr** established rate
- **2-3** completed engagements

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

### Week 1: Profile Setup

**Day 1-2: Expert Networks**

- [ ] Apply to [Guidepoint](https://guidepoint.com) - Often accepts within 48 hours
- [ ] Apply to [GLG](https://glg.it) - Best for senior professionals
- [ ] Apply to [AlphaSights](https://alphasights.com) - High volume opportunities

**Day 3-4: Professional Presence**

- [ ] Update LinkedIn headline: "Cybersecurity Consultant | [Your Specialty] | Available for Advisory"
- [ ] Add "Open to consulting" in your profile settings
- [ ] Create 1-page consulting bio (use for applications)

**Day 5-7: Legal/Admin**

- [ ] Research consulting tax requirements in your jurisdiction
- [ ] Set up separate bank account for consulting income
- [ ] Draft simple consulting agreement template

**Deliverables:**

- 3 expert network applications submitted
- LinkedIn optimized for consulting
- Basic admin structure in place

### Week 2: Platform Expansion

**Day 8-10: More Expert Networks**

- [ ] Apply to [Third Bridge](https://thirdbridge.com)
- [ ] Apply to [Coleman Research](https://colemanrg.com)
- [ ] Apply to [Maven](https://maven.co) (good for tech expertise)

**Day 11-14: Specialized Platforms**

_If you have GRC experience:_

- [ ] Apply to [SideChannel](https://sidechannel.com) for vCISO work
- [ ] Apply to [FRSecure](https://frsecure.com)

_If you have pentest experience:_

- [ ] Apply to [Synack Red Team](https://synack.com)
- [ ] Create [Bugcrowd](https://bugcrowd.com) profile

_If you have DFIR experience:_

- [ ] Join DFIR-focused Slack/Discord communities
- [ ] Research IR retainer opportunities

**Deliverables:**

- 5+ total platform applications
- Specialty platforms identified
- Community connections started

### Week 3: Rate Calibration

**Day 15-17: Research**

- [ ] Review rate guides in this repository
- [ ] Set initial rate: **$250-$300/hr** for expert networks
- [ ] Document 3 rate increase triggers

**Day 18-21: First Calls**

- [ ] Accept first expert network consultation (at your set rate)
- [ ] Complete call professionally
- [ ] Request feedback/rating if available

**Rate Setting Framework:**

| Experience Level | Expert Network Rate | vCISO Monthly |
| ---------------- | ------------------- | ------------- |
| 5-8 years        | $200-$300/hr        | $3K-$5K       |
| 8-12 years       | $300-$450/hr        | $5K-$10K      |
| 12+ years        | $450-$700/hr        | $10K-$15K     |

**Deliverables:**

- Rate established and documented
- First consultation completed
- Feedback collected

### Week 4: Content Creation

**Day 22-25: Case Studies**

- [ ] Write 2-3 sanitized case studies from your experience
- [ ] Focus on outcomes: "Reduced incident response time by 60%"
- [ ] Format for use in proposals and profiles

**Day 26-30: Thought Leadership**

- [ ] Publish 1 LinkedIn article on your specialty
- [ ] Comment on 5+ relevant posts daily
- [ ] Connect with 10+ potential clients/referrers

**Deliverables:**

- 2-3 case studies ready
- 1 published article
- Active LinkedIn presence

---

## Phase 2: Acceleration (Days 31-60)

### Week 5-6: Volume Building

**Goals:**

- Complete 4-6 expert network calls
- Target $1,500-$3,000 in earnings
- Build platform reputation

**Daily Actions:**

- [ ] Check platform notifications 2x/day
- [ ] Respond to opportunities within 2 hours
- [ ] Accept calls that match your expertise (don't stretch too far)

**Conversion Tips:**

- Be available for short-notice calls (premium rates available)
- Follow up with clients after calls
- Ask for referrals to other projects

### Week 7-8: Specialization

**Identify Your Niche:**

Choose based on your strongest experience:

- **DFIR + Memory Forensics** → IR retainers, expert witness prep
- **GRC + Compliance** → vCISO, audit support
- **Penetration Testing** → Bug bounty, red team assessments
- **Cloud Security** → Architecture reviews, compliance
- **Detection Engineering** → SIEM optimization, threat hunting

**Actions:**

- [ ] Update all profiles with niche focus
- [ ] Create niche-specific service package
- [ ] Identify 10 target companies in your niche

**Example Package:**

> **Detection Engineering Sprint**
>
> - 40 hours over 4 weeks
> - SIEM rule optimization
> - 10 custom detection rules
> - Threat hunting playbook
> - **Price: $15,000-$20,000**

### Week 8: Rate Increase

**If you've completed 5+ calls successfully:**

- [ ] Raise rate by $50-$100/hr for new opportunities
- [ ] Keep existing client rates stable
- [ ] Document rate in all profiles

**Target Phase 2 Earnings: $3,000-$6,000 total**

---

## Phase 3: Optimization (Days 61-90)

### Week 9-10: Retainer Pursuit

**Goal:** Secure 1 recurring engagement

**Actions:**

- [ ] Reach out to 5 potential vCISO clients via LinkedIn
- [ ] Propose "security advisory retainer" to interested parties
- [ ] Offer 3-month trial at reduced rate to build trust

**Retainer Template:**

> **Monthly Security Advisory Retainer**
>
> - 8 hours/month guaranteed availability
> - Weekly 30-min check-in call
> - Unlimited email consultation
> - Priority incident response
> - **Price: $4,000-$8,000/month**

### Week 11-12: Pipeline Building

**Long-term Revenue:**

- [ ] Apply to 2 expert witness platforms (if qualified)
- [ ] Create course outline for Udemy/Skillshare (future income)
- [ ] Build referral relationships with 3 other consultants

**Expert Witness Platforms:**

- [Expert Institute](https://expertinstitute.com)
- [Round Table Group](https://roundtablegroup.com)
- [SEAK](https://seak.com) (training required)

- [ ] Set Year 1 income target

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Underpricing** - Start at market rate, not below
2. **Overcommitting** - Don't take calls outside your expertise
3. **Neglecting follow-up** - Always thank clients and ask for referrals
4. **Ignoring admin** - Track time and invoice promptly
5. **No niche** - Specialists earn 2-3x generalists
