# Comprehensive List of Expert Network Platforms (2025)

## Overview
This is a complete list of expert network platforms similar to GLG, Arbolus, and Guidepoint. These platforms connect professionals like you with clients (investors, consulting firms, corporations) who need expert insights.

## Top 10 Major Expert Networks

- **Founded:** 1998
- **Network Size:** 800,000+ freelance consultants

## How to Register: General Process

Most expert networks follow these steps:

### Step 1: Prepare Your Profile
- Update LinkedIn profile with current role, expertise, and achievements
- Prepare a professional bio (2-3 sentences)
- Gather resume/CV
- List 3-5 areas of expertise
- Identify industries and companies you know

### Step 2: Apply to Networks
Visit each platform's website and look for:
- "Join as Expert" button
- "Become an Expert" link
- "Apply Now" option
- Contact form for expert applications

### Step 3: Complete Application
Typically includes:
- Email address
- Full name and background
- Professional experience
- Areas of expertise
- CV/resume upload
- LinkedIn profile link

### Step 4: Screening Process
- Phone or video interview (15-30 minutes)
- Discuss your expertise and availability
- Confirm your hourly rate
- Answer screening questions

### Step 5: Onboarding
- Review compliance agreements
- Understand confidentiality requirements
- Set up your expert profile
- Indicate availability and hourly rates
- Complete any training modules

### Step 6: Start Receiving Opportunities
- Check your inbox for project invitations
- Respond quickly to match with clients
- Complete project details
- Schedule calls with clients

## Tips for Registration Success

1. **Complete Your Profile:** The more detailed, the better. Include:
   - Specific companies you've worked for
   - Exact products/services you know
   - Competitors you understand
   - Current role and responsibilities

2. **Set Realistic Rates:** Typical ranges:
   - Entry-level/junior: $100-$200/hour
   - Mid-level/managers: $200-$400/hour
   - Senior executives: $400-$1,000+/hour

3. **Be Responsive:** Networks prioritize experts who respond within hours to opportunities

4. **Keep Profile Updated:** Update with new companies, products, roles as your career changes

5. **Join Multiple Networks:** There's no penalty for joining multiple platforms simultaneously

6. **Optimize LinkedIn:** Networks use LinkedIn heavily to find experts
   - Use specific keywords in headline
   - Detail tools/software you know
   - Add publications and presentations
   - Get recommendations from colleagues

7. **Be Proactive:** If not contacted, reach out to networks via their contact forms

## Important Notes

1. **Multiple Network Membership:** You can and should join multiple expert networks simultaneously
2. **No Fees:** There are no fees to join or maintain expert status
3. **Invite-Only:** Some networks are invite-only (like Third Bridge), but you can express interest
4. **Exclusivity:** Most networks (except Capvision historically) don't prevent you from joining competitors
5. **Compliance:** All require compliance training on confidentiality and insider trading rules
6. **Payment Methods:** Vary by network (bank transfer, PayPal, WeChat, Alipay, etc.)
