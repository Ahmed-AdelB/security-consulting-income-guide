## Firm Deep Dives

### Bishop Fox
- Rate signal: $250-$600/hr in the platform tracker.
- Project types: elite offensive security, red teaming, advanced penetration testing, custom attack simulations, high-stakes clients.
- Application process: high difficulty; practical offensive skills emphasized; CTF/home labs/blogs preferred; 38 interview questions referenced.
- Career growth: Bishop Fox Academy (BFA) with 155-skill matrix; apprentice â†’ managing consultant ladder.
- Compensation context: consultant pay $98K-$175K (avg $118K base + $13K); compensation rating 5.0/5.
- Culture notes: remote-friendly; training budget; work-life balance requires boundaries; limited internal mobility noted.
- Contact channels: `careers@bishopfox.com`, `contact@bishopfox.com`.

### NCC Group
- Rate signal: $200-$500/hr in the platform tracker; contractor rate not public; employee avg $128,731/year ($62/hr).
- Project types: penetration testing scenarios show up in interview assessments; general cybersecurity consulting.
- Application process: medium-high difficulty; multi-stage interviews; code translation + HTTP/TCP/UDP fundamentals; 21.43 days average.
- Compensation context: security consultant avg $144,214; senior $187,496-$255,889 total; comp rating 2.8/5; WLB 3.7/5.
- Risk flags: four rounds of layoffs, offshoring, restructuring through May 2025, morale decline.
- Contact channels: `response@nccgroup.com`, `Investor_Relations@nccgroup.com`.

### Rapid7
- Rate signal: $200-$450/hr in the platform tracker.
- Project types: consulting services tied to product ecosystem; services org described as marketing division by reviewers.
- Application process: no firm-specific interview data located in repo sources.
- Compensation context: security consultant $120K-$199K (avg $136K base + $18K); comp rating 3.5/5.
- Culture notes: unlimited PTO and good balance cited, but high turnover, churn-and-burn culture, long hours noted.
- Contact channels: `sales@rapid7.com`, `investors@rapid7.com`, `press@rapid7.com`.

### CrowdStrike (Crowdstrike Services)
- Rate signal: $250-$600/hr in the platform tracker.
- Project types: incident response specialist best-fit; large enterprise consulting services.
- Application process: no firm-specific interview data located in repo sources.
- Compensation context: overall rating 4.0/5; WLB 4.3/5; compensation rating 4.0/5; pay "pretty good" with quarterly bonuses.
- Culture notes: remote-first with strong benefits; concerns about reorgs and internal promotion gaps.
- Contact channels: `sales@crowdstrike.com`, `investors@crowdstrike.com`, `press@crowdstrike.com`.
