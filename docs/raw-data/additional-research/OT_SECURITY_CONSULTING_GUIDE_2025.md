# OT Security Consulting Guide 2025

## Scope

This guide consolidates IoT/OT/ICS/SCADA consulting income benchmarks from the existing internal research library. It focuses on contractor hourly rates, specialty premiums, and market positioning for 2025.

## Executive Summary

- OT/ICS consulting commands premium hourly rates, with documented bands from **$275-$550/hr** up to **$500-$800+/hr** depending on seniority and specialization.
- Specialized OT/ICS providers in the platform list show **$300-$700/hr** project rates for IEC 62443 and 5G/telecom-adjacent work.
- OT/ICS specialization carries a **+40-60% premium** compared with general security consulting, while manufacturing/industrial industry work adds **+20-40%** in compensation uplift.
- Full-time OT/ICS salaries are modeled at **$125K-$220K**, with projections reaching **$160K-$280K** by 2030 as demand accelerates.

## Compensation Benchmarks (Annual)

- **Current OT/ICS salary range:** $125K-$220K.
- **Projected OT/ICS salary range (2030):** $160K-$280K.
- **Demand signal:** OT/ICS roles require NIST 800-82, IEC 62443, and NERC CIP expertise, with rising attack volumes driving hiring.

## Premium Multipliers & Industry Effects

- **OT/ICS specialization premium:** +40-60% above general security consulting rates.
- **Manufacturing/industrial premium:** +20-40% average compensation uplift tied to OT/ICS risk.
- **Contractor uplift guidance:** 1099 consultants typically price 25-30% above equivalent W-2 rates to cover taxes, benefits, and downtime.

## Engagement Models & Pricing Anchors

These ranges are general security consulting anchors that OT/ICS work typically exceeds by the OT premium above.

- **Penetration tests (industrial focus):** $25K-$150K.
- **Incident response retainer:** $50K-$200K/year.
- **Monthly advisory retainer:** $3K-$15K/month.
