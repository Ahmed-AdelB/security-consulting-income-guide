## 4. RECOMMENDATIONS

1.  **For Job Seekers:** **HackerOne** and **CrowdStrike** offer the best work environments, though CrowdStrike's recent layoffs warrant a stability check during interviews.
2.  **For High Earners:** **Bishop Fox** and **Mandiant** (Google) remain the kings of compensation for consulting roles.
3.  **For Contractors:** **GLG** is the volume leader for experts, but **HackerOne** offers a better community and culture for security researchers.
4.  **Avoid:** **Bugcrowd** until cultural issues are addressed, and **AlphaSights** if you value personal time.
