## Rate & Positioning Strategy

### Expert Networks
- **Start rate:** $500-$600/hr to build call history.
- **Raise after 10 calls:** $700-$800/hr.
- **Premium niche rate:** $800-$1,000/hr for 5G, GRC, cloud, zero trust.
- **Tactical edge:** Reply to requests within 3 hours; rate negotiation is expected.

### vCISO Retainers
- **Core packages:** $5K (starter), $8K (growth), $12K+ (enterprise).
- **Target capacity:** 3-5 clients for $25K/month baseline.
- **Lead magnet:** 30-day security assessment + roadmap.

### Expert Witness
- **Base rate:** $400-$800/hr with $2K-$5K retainers.
- **Billing:** Charge for prep, document review, deposition, and testimony.
- **Credibility:** Maintain a clear conflict-of-interest log and updated CV.

### Bug Bounty & Web3
- **Bug bounty:** Prioritize private programs and high-severity targets.
- **Web3 audits:** Build a portfolio with 1-2 contest writeups; target top-10% placements.
- **Consistency:** 2-3 focused targets per week beats broad scanning.
