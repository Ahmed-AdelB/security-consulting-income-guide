## Quick Start by Experience Level

=== "Junior (0-3 years)"

    1. Focus on **bug bounty** platforms to build portfolio
    2. Join **Synack Red Team** for structured income
    3. Apply to 5 expert networks with niche focus
    4. Target **$5K-$15K/month** in Year 1

=== "Mid-Level (3-7 years)"

    1. Maximize **expert network** calls at $400-$600/hr
    2. Start building **vCISO client base** (2-3 clients)
    3. Get listed on expert witness directories
    4. Target **$15K-$40K/month** in Year 1

=== "Senior (7+ years)"

    1. Premium **expert network** rates at $800-$1,500/hr
    2. Scale **vCISO practice** to 4-6 clients
    3. Active **expert witness** work ($600+/hr)
    4. Target **$50K-$100K/month** in Year 1
