    Compensation Trends (2025)¶

    Role Entry Level Mid-Level Senior Principal/Director
    Security Consultant $80K-$110K $110K-$150K $150K-$200K $200K-$300K
    Penetration Tester $70K-$100K $100K-$140K $140K-$180K $180K-$250K
    Security Architect $100K-$130K $130K-$170K $170K-$220K $220K-$350K
    vCISO (Consulting) - $150K-$250K $250K-$400K $400K-$600K+

    Hourly Rate Benchmarks¶

    Expert Network Calls $200-$400 $400-$700 $700-$1,200 $1,200-$2,000
    Penetration Testing $100-$175 $175-$275 $275-$400 $400-$600
    vCISO Services $150-$250 $250-$400 $400-$600 $600-$1,000
    Expert Witness $200-$400 $400-$600 $600-$900 $900-$1,500
