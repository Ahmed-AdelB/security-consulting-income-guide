    CODEX MONTHLY ACTION PLAN: PATH TO $100K (YEAR 1)¶

    Purpose: A tactical, month-by-month execution guide to reaching $100,000/month in cybersecurity consulting revenue within 12 months. This plan synthesizes the "Ultimate Playbook" and "Final Strategy" into immediate monthly actions.

    üóì MONTHLY EXECUTION PROTOCOLS¶

    üü¢ MONTH 1: FOUNDATION & ACTIVATION¶

    Goal: Activate all "Fast Cashflow" channels (Expert Networks) and plant seeds for Retainers. Revenue Target: $16,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Profile Sprint: Finalize Master Profile, 1-Page Bio, and Expert Witness CV. - [ ] Application Blitz (Tier 1): Apply to GLG, AlphaSights, Coleman, Guidepoint, Third Bridge. - [ ] Application Blitz (vCISO): Apply to SideChannel, The vCISO Directory, Pivot Point Security. - [ ] Application Blitz (Witness): Apply to Round Table Group, Expert Institute, IMS Legal. - [ ] Rate Setting: Set initial expert network rates at $400-$500/hr. Do not negotiate yet‚Äîget accepted. - [ ] Speed Rule: Respond to all expert network invites within 3 hours. - [ ] Referral Launch: Contact 5 MSPs/MSSPs to propose a white-label vCISO partnership. - [ ] Bug Bounty: Register on HackerOne/Synack; complete verified profiles.

    Key Platforms: GLG, AlphaSights, SideChannel, Round Table Group.

    üü¢ MONTH 2: VALIDATION & FIRST RATES¶

    Goal: Secure first repeated paid calls and land the first vCISO retainer. Revenue Target: $30,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Rate Negotiation: After 3 paid calls on any network, request a rate hike to $600/hr. - [ ] vCISO Pitching: Send 10 proposals for "30-Day Security Assessment" (paid entry to retainer). - [ ] Content Engine: Publish 1 case study ("How I Saved [Client] $X or Prevented Y Breach"). - [ ] Credibility: Post "Available for Expert Witness Work" on LinkedIn with specific niche tags. - [ ] Follow-up: Re-ping all MSP contacts from Month 1 with a specific "Incident Response Readiness" offer. - [ ] Regional Expansion: Apply to MENA/APAC networks (StrategyConnect, Mavensworld) for overnight coverage.

    Key Platforms: Third Bridge, Guidepoint, FRSecure, JurisPro.

    üü¢ MONTH 3: RETAINER #1 & SYSTEMIZATION¶

    Goal: Lock first recurring revenue ($5k-$8k/mo) to cover baseline expenses. Revenue Target: $41,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Close Retainer #1: Convert an assessment client to a $5,000/mo recurring vCISO package. - [ ] Tier 2 Activation: Apply to IANS Research, Mosaic, and specialized networks (Tegus, Dialectica). - [ ] Contest Entry: Enter 1 Code4rena or Sherlock audit contest (Web3) to build leaderboard rep. - [ ] Asset Prep: Create standard "Board Report Template" and "Policy Deck" to speed up vCISO delivery. - [ ] Feedback Loop: Ask every expert network client for a 5-star rating to boost algorithm ranking.

    Key Platforms: IANS Research, RealCISO, Code4rena, SEAK.

    üü° MONTH 4: EXPANSION & WORKSHOPS¶

    Goal: Diversify into Training/Speaking to build authority and add "lumpy" cash. Revenue Target: $53,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Workshop Launch: Pitch a $5,000 "Executive Cyber Risk Workshop" to current or past clients. - [ ] Expert Witness: Follow up on legal directories; aim for first case inquiry. - [ ] Rate Hike #2: Push top-performing expert networks to $700-$800/hr. - [ ] De-Risking: If any single client > 50% of income, aggressively prospect for Retainer #2. - [ ] Bug Bounty: Dedicate 5 hours/week to a private program on HackerOne (higher ROI than public).

    Key Platforms: SANS (Instructor application), LinkedIn Learning, HackerOne Private Programs.

    üü° MONTH 5: RETAINER #2 & CASE INTAKE¶

    Goal: Double the recurring revenue floor. Revenue Target: $64,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Close Retainer #2: Target is $8,000/mo (Mid-Tier Package). - [ ] Legal Sales: Reach out to 10 "Cyber Insurance Panel Counsel" law firms with your CV. - [ ] Content Repurposing: Turn Month 4's workshop into a recorded course outline. - [ ] Efficiency: Hire a VA or junior contractor to handle scheduling and basic research.

    Key Platforms: The Expert Institute, Maven, Cobalt (PTaaS).

    üü° MONTH 6: HALFWAY POINT (SCALE)¶

    Goal: Optimize delivery; drop low-paying clients/platforms. Revenue Target: $74,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] 80/20 Audit: Review all income sources. Fire the bottom 20% paying clients/platforms. - [ ] Retainer #3 Pipeline: Start conversations for the next vCISO slot. - [ ] Speaker Reel: Update speaker profile with clips from Month 4 workshop. - [ ] Web3 Focus: Shift from "contests" to "private audit" invites if leaderboard rank is high enough.

    Key Platforms: Spearbit, SpeakerHub, Sessionize.

    üî¥ MONTH 7: RETAINER #3 & HIGH RATES¶

    Goal: Approach the $100k mark; recurring revenue should now cover ~50% of target. Revenue Target: $91,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Close Retainer #3: Target $12,000/mo (Premium Package). - [ ] Rate Standardization: All new expert network requests must be $800/hr+. - [ ] Course Pre-Sale: Launch pre-sales for the online course (passive income stream).

    Key Platforms: Synack (Red Team), TechCXO.

    üî¥ MONTH 8: THE $100K BREAKTHROUGH¶

    Goal: Hit the six-figure monthly run rate. Revenue Target: $105,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Sprint: Maximum availability for expert networks (aim for 25 hours @ $800 = $20k). - [ ] Retainers: Ensure 3 clients are active and paying ($25k total). - [ ] Big Wins: Land 1 major Expert Witness retainer ($10k) or High-Severity Bug Bounty ($10k). - [ ] Training: Deliver 2 corporate workshops ($15k total). - [ ] Review: Celebrate, then assess sustainability. Don't burn out.

    Target Mix: vCISO ($45k) + Expert Net ($25k) + Witness ($15k) + Training ($15k) + Bounties ($5k).

    üîµ MONTH 9: STABILIZATION & UPSELL¶

    Goal: Stabilize after the sprint; focus on upselling existing retainers. Revenue Target: $114,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Upsell: Pitch "Tabletop Exercises" or "Penetration Test" add-ons to vCISO clients. - [ ] Course Launch: Release the full online course (replace active workshop time). - [ ] Delegation: Use the contractor to offload 50% of the "grunt work" (reporting, scanning).

    üîµ MONTH 10: OPTIMIZATION¶

    Goal: Refine the machine; focus purely on high-margin work. Revenue Target: $120,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Rate Hike #3: Test $1,000/hr on top-tier niche expert networks. - [ ] Retainer Review: Renew 6-month contracts; increase prices by 20%. - [ ] Thought Leadership: Publish a "State of the Industry" report to drive inbound leads.

    üîµ MONTH 11: RETAINER #5 OR BIG CASE¶

    Goal: Push for a record month or prepare for year-end renewals. Revenue Target: $129,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Expansion: Sign Retainer #4 or #5 (waitlist only). - [ ] Expert Witness: Aim for a trial testimony appearance (high daily rate). - [ ] Partnerships: Formalize referral deal with a top MSP (10% commission).

    üîµ MONTH 12: STEADY STATE¶

    Goal: Establish a sustainable rhythm for Year 2. Revenue Target: $143,000

    Action Checklist: - [ ] Year-End Review: Calculate effective hourly rate across all streams. - [ ] Tax/Legal: Ensure entity structure and retirement accounts are optimized for $1M+ revenue. - [ ] Year 2 Plan: Set goal for $2M/year or reduced hours for same income.
