    Would you be open to a brief call to discuss how I might contribute to your client projects?

    Best regards, [Your Name] [LinkedIn Profile Link]

    3. Rate Negotiation & Contracting¶

    3.1 Turning Down a Lowball Rate¶

    Subject: Re: Proposal for [Project]

    Hi [Client Name],

    Thanks for the transparency on the budget.

    Unfortunately, at $[Rate]/hour, we are too far apart for me to deliver the level of rigor and senior oversight this specific project requires. My typical engagements start at the ranges outlined in the proposal to ensure we don't miss critical risks.

    I might be able to refer you to a more junior consultant who fits that budget range if you'd like? Otherwise, if the budget opens up in the future for a deeper dive, let me know.

    Best, [Your Name]

    3.2 The Annual Rate Increase¶

    Subject: 2026 Rate Schedule & Partnership Update

    Hi [Client Name],

    I'm writing to share a quick update on our partnership for the coming year. First, I've really enjoyed working on [Project/Success] this year.

    As my practice has grown and I've invested in new [Certifications/Tooling/Capabilities], I am updating my standard rate schedule for 2026.

    Effective [Date - 30-60 days out], my hourly rate will move to $[New Rate]/hour (from $[Old Rate]).

    However, for long-term partners like you, I want to keep your current rate locked in for an additional 3 months as a thank you. Alternatively, if you'd like to lock in the current rate for the full year, we can switch to a pre-paid retainer model.

    Let me know if you'd like to explore that.

    Best, [Your Name]

    3.3 Confirming Cancellation Terms (Tegus/Networks)¶

    Subject: Confirmation of Compensation for Cancellations

    Hi [Associate Name],

    Before I accept the call, can you confirm the cancellation policy in writing?

    Specifically, I want to confirm compensation if the client cancels within 24 hours of the scheduled time or if the call ends early. Please also confirm any prorating rules (e.g., is it prorated to the minute or 15-minute increments?).

    Thanks, [Your Name]

    3.4 Clarifying Transcript Usage¶

    Subject: Transcript Usage Confirmation

    Hi [Associate Name],

    I understand [Platform] records and transcribes calls for the client library.

    Please confirm when transcripts are released and whether any sections can be redacted on request. I will keep responses compliance-safe, but want clarity on the library access scope before proceeding.

    Best, [Your Name]
