    CODEX MASTER IMPLEMENTATION GUIDE (Step-by-Step to $100K/Month)¶

    1. Executive Summary¶

    This Master Guide consolidates all research from the "CODEX 10K" series into a single, linear execution plan. The Goal: Reach $100,000/month in diverse cybersecurity consulting revenue within 12-18 months. The Strategy: High-Rate Expert Network Calls + Recurring vCISO Retainers + Expert Witness Upside + Strategic Web3/Content.

    2. The $100K/Month Income Mix¶

    To hit $100K/month, you do not need 160 hours of billable time. You need a blended rate of ~$625/hr.

    Stream Monthly Target Activity Required
    Expert Networks $55,000 ~22 hours/week (88 hrs/mo) @ $625/hr
    vCISO Retainers $25,000 4 clients @ $6,250/mo (Total ~40-60 hrs/mo)
    Expert Witness $8,000 ~1 active case/mo (10 hours @ $800/hr)
    Bug Bounty/Web3 $6,000 1-2 critical/high findings or audit contests
    Training/Content $6,000 1 workshop/mo ($5K) + Passive Royalties ($1K)
    TOTAL $100,000 Diverse, Resilient, Scalable

    3. Phase 1: Foundation & Activation (Weeks 1-4)¶

    Objective: Plant seeds across all high-value platforms and secure first "quick wins" (Expert Network calls).

    Week 1: The "Big 8" Applications¶

    Focus: Apply to the highest volume/highest rate platforms immediately. 1. GLG (Gerson Lehrman Group): Create profile. Rate: $500/hr. 2. AlphaSights: Create profile. Rate: $500/hr. 3. Coleman Research: Create profile. Rate: $500/hr. 4. SideChannel: Apply for vCISO roles. 5. The vCISO Directory: Create a free listing. 6. Round Table Group: Register for Expert Witness work. 7. Expert Institute: Register for Expert Witness work. 8. HackerOne: Update profile for private programs.

    Week 2: Expansion & Outreach¶

    Focus: Second tier networks and direct outreach. 1. Third Bridge: Apply. Rate: $500/hr. 2. Guidepoint: Apply. Rate: $500/hr. 3. Maven: Apply. Rate: $400/hr. 4. Pivot Point Security: Apply for vCISO sub-contracting. 5. FRSecure: Apply for vCISO sub-contracting. 6. IMS Legal Strategies: Register for Expert Witness. 7. JurisPro: Create profile ($$).

    Weeks 3-4: Niche & Web3¶

    Focus: Specialized high-rate networks and crypto. 1. IANS Research: Apply (Cybersecurity specialized). Rate: $600/hr. 2. Mosaic Research: Apply. 3. RealCISO: Create profile. 4. SEAK: Research training/certification for Expert Witness. 5. Code4rena / Sherlock: Register for Web3 audit contests. 6. Spearbit: Apply to guild.

    Phase 1 Deliverables: * [ ] Master Bio/CV created (Generic, vCISO specific, Expert Witness specific). * [ ] 20+ Applications submitted. * [ ] First Expert Network call completed.

    4. Phase 2: Acceleration & Retainers (Months 2-4)¶

    Objective: Turn "calls" into "contracts" and secure recurring revenue (vCISO).

    Month 2: The First Retainer¶

    • Action: Follow up on all vCISO applications.
    • Action: Negotiate Expert Network rates. If you did 3 calls at $500, ask for $600 for the next.
    • Goal: Secure 1 vCISO Retainer ($2,500 - $5,000/mo).
    • Metric: Monthly Revenue Run-rate: $15k - $30k.

    Month 3: Expert Witness & Web3¶

    • Action: Land first Expert Witness inquiry (via Round Table or IMS).
    • Action: Enter 1 Code4rena/Sherlock contest. Aim for "Medium" findings to build rep.
    • Goal: Add vCISO Retainer #2.
    • Metric: Monthly Revenue Run-rate: $40k - $50k.

    Month 4: Stabilization¶

    • Action: Raising Expert Network base rate to $600-$700/hr on all platforms.
    • Action: Audit pipeline. Drop platforms with 0 leads. Double down on top 5.
    • Goal: Consistent 10+ Expert Network calls/month.

    5. Phase 3: Diversification & Scaling (Months 5-8)¶

    Objective: Hit the $100K month by stacking high-leverage income.

    Month 5: High-Ticket Training¶

    • Action: Develop a "Signature Workshop" (e.g., "Board Cybersecurity Reporting").
    • Action: Pitch this to current vCISO clients as an add-on ($5k/session).
    • Goal: $5k-$10k in Training revenue.

    Month 6: The Rate Hike¶

    • Action: Expert Network Rate Target: $800-$1,000/hr.
    • Action: vCISO Retainer Target: $8,000/mo (Enterprise Tier).
    • Goal: Replace low-paying vCISO client with a high-paying one.

    Month 7-8: The "Perfect Month"¶

    • Combine:
      • 20 Expert Calls ($16k)
      • 4 Retainers ($30k)
      • 1 Major Expert Witness Case ($20k retainer)
      • 1 Critical Bug Bounty/Audit ($15k)
      • 1 Workshop ($5k)
      • TOTAL: ~$86k (Approaching target).

    7. The Golden Rules of $100K/Month¶

    1. The 3-Hour Rule: Respond to Expert Network invites within 3 hours. Speed = Selection.
    2. Always Negotiate: Never accept the default rate after your first 5 calls. "My standard rate is now $X."
    3. Retainers > Hours: Never do hourly consulting for clients directly. Sell monthly outcomes (Retainers). Hourly is only for Expert Networks/Witness.
    4. Cross-Pollinate: Use Expert Network insights to impress vCISO clients. Use vCISO "war stories" to win Expert Witness cases.
    5. No Free Work: Discovery calls are 15 mins. Anything more is a paid assessment.

    8. Weekly Maintenance Checklist¶

    • Monday: Check all Expert Network dashboards. Apply to 5 new relevant projects.
    • Tuesday: vCISO Client work / Board Meetings.
    • Wednesday: Deep Work (Web3 Audit or Content Creation).
    • Thursday: Business Development (Follow up on proposals, network with MSPs).
    • Friday: Administrative (Invoicing, Scheduling next week).
