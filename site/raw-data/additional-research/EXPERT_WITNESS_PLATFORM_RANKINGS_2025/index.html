    Expert Witness Platform Rankings 2025 - Quick Reference¶

    1. Expert Institute - Active AI-powered matching, 100% fees
    2. Round Table Group - Prestigious referral service, AmLaw 200 reach

    1. SEAK Directory - If you value training ($1,395-$1,595) + directory ($545)
    2. JurisPro - Only if LexisNexis reach important ($399/year)

    Decision Matrix: Which Platforms for You?¶

    New Expert (0-2 years testimony)¶

    Start Here:

    • Expert Institute (free)
    • Round Table Group (free)
    • SEAK Training Program ($1,395) - HIGHLY RECOMMENDED
    • SEAK Directory ($545) - if training purchased

    Avoid:

    • Paid directories without training value
    • Multiple paid platforms simultaneously
    • Premium consulting firms (not yet qualified)

    Expected Timeline: 12-18 months to establish case flow

    Established Expert (3-10 years)¶

    Maintain:

    • Expert Institute (ongoing cases)
    • Round Table Group (premium opportunities)

    Add Selectively:

    • SEAK Directory ($545) - for fee data + visibility
    • JurisPro ($399) - if budget allows
    • ForensisGroup - apply for vetting
    • IMS Legal Strategies - build relationship

    Optimize:

    • Raise rates to $500-$750/hour
    • Implement $5,000 retainers
    • Specialize niche further

    Expected Timeline: 3-6 months between cases

    Elite Expert (10+ years, high-profile)¶

    Strategic Platform Use:

    • Expert Institute (passive income)
    • Round Table Group (selective)
    • IMS Legal Strategies (bet-the-company cases)

    Career Options:

    • FTI Consulting (employment) - #1 global firm
    • Ankura (employment) - Forbes-ranked
    • Build independent consulting firm
    • Transition to corporate executive role

    Optimize:

    • Rates: $1,000-$2,500+/hour
    • Retainers: $10,000-$25,000+
    • Selective case acceptance (5-15 high-value/year)

    Red Flags & Green Flags¶

    Platform Red Flags (Avoid)¶

    • Requires payment before any case opportunities
    • Promises guaranteed case volume
    • Markup/commission not disclosed upfront
    • Pressure to lower rates
    • Attorneys can't contact you directly
    • No track record or references
    • "Too good to be true" marketing

    Platform Green Flags (Pursue)¶

    • Free or transparent fee structure
    • Established track record (5+ years)
    • Direct attorney contact
    • 100% fee retention
    • No case obligations
    • Reputable law firm clients
    • Clear vetting process
    • Industry recognition

    Cybersecurity Expert Witness Checklist¶

    Required Credentials¶

    • CISSP or CEH certification (minimum)
    • 5+ years cybersecurity experience
    • Active professional practice (not retired)
    • Professional liability insurance
    • Clean background (no conflicts)

    Highly Recommended¶

    • Prior expert witness testimony
    • Published articles/research
    • Conference presentations
    • Professional association membership (SIA, CIS, CSA)
    • Specialized niche (e.g., "ransomware forensics" vs. "cybersecurity")
    • Advanced degree (MS/PhD preferred)

    Nice to Have¶

    • Media appearances
    • Book authorship
    • Industry awards
    • Former law enforcement/government
    • Academic affiliation
    • Multiple certifications

    Common Mistakes to Avoid¶

    Mistake #1: Over-Investing Too Early¶

    Problem: Spending $2,000+ on paid directories before establishing track record Solution: Start with 100% free platforms (Expert Institute, Round Table Group)

    Mistake #2: Generic Positioning¶

    Problem: Listing as "cybersecurity expert" (too broad) Solution: Niche down to "cloud security forensics" or "BIPA compliance expert"

    Mistake #3: No Written Agreement¶

    Problem: Starting work without retention agreement = non-payment risk Solution: NEVER begin work without signed agreement and retainer

    Mistake #4: Underpricing¶

    Problem: Charging $150-200/hour signals low credibility Solution: Research SEAK data, start at $350-400/hour minimum

    Mistake #5: Passive Approach¶

    Problem: Just listing on directories and waiting Solution: Active networking, speaking, publishing, attorney relationship-building

    3. Get: CISSP or CEH (if not already certified)
