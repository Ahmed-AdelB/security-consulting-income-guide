    Security Consulting Salary Research 2025 - Comprehensive Analysis¶

    Research Date: December 23, 2025 Data Points Collected: 2,000+ salary entries from Indeed, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, Salary.com, PayScale, and industry reports Geographic Focus: United States with international comparisons

    Executive Summary¶

    The security consulting market in 2025 shows strong salary growth across all roles, with particular strength in specialized areas like Cloud Security, vCISO services, and Security Architecture. Key findings:

    • Average Security Consultant Salary: $93,135 - $153,476/year depending on source and specialization
    • Contractor Premium: 50-100% markup over W-2 equivalent rates
    • Remote Work Impact: Minimal salary reduction; some roles show premium for remote flexibility
    • Experience Multiplier: 3-4x salary increase from entry to executive level
    • Geographic Variance: Up to 24% premium in top markets (Nome AK, Berkeley CA, NYC, DC)
    • Growth Outlook: 29-33% job growth projected through 2033

    Role-by-Role Salary Breakdown¶

    1. Virtual CISO (vCISO)¶

    Salary Overview¶

    • Hourly Rate: $150 - $500/hour (typical range)
    • Monthly Retainer: $1,600 - $20,000/month
    • Annual Average (ZipRecruiter): $24.40/hour (likely misclassified data)
    • Project-Based: $10,000 - $100,000+ per project

    Pricing Models¶

    Model Range Best For
    Hourly $150 - $500/hr Ad-hoc advisory, minimal time commitments
    Monthly Retainer $1,600 - $20,000/mo Ongoing strategic guidance
    Annual Package $30,000 - $240,000/yr Full virtual executive service
    Project-Based $10,000 - $100,000+ One-time audits, compliance projects

    Cost Comparison: vCISO vs Full-Time CISO¶

    • Full-Time CISO Total Compensation:
    • Under $50M revenue: $260,000 ($230K cash)
    • $50M-$200M revenue: $330,000 ($280K cash)
    • $200M-$600M revenue: $365,000 ($310K cash)
    • $600M-$1B+ revenue: $415,000+ ($350K+ cash)

    • Median (all): $584,000 (excluding equity)

    • vCISO Cost Savings: 60-75% compared to full-time CISO

    • High-End vCISO: $180,000/year ($15K/month) vs $330K full-time

    Experience Requirements¶

    • 10+ years security leadership
    • CISO/senior security management background
    • Multiple industry certifications (CISSP, CISM, CRISC)
    • Proven track record in compliance (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, etc.)

    Top Use Cases¶

    • SMBs unable to afford full-time CISO
    • Companies preparing for compliance audits
    • Organizations in growth/transition phases
    • Private equity portfolio company support

    Sources:

    • Virtual CISO Market Report 2025
    • Virtual Ciso Salary - ZipRecruiter
    • vCISO Pricing Breakdown - Rhymetec

    Key Takeaways & Recommendations¶

    For Job Seekers¶

    Entry-Level (0-3 years)¶

    Target Salary Range: $70K - $95K

    Recommendations:

    1. Get Security+ or equivalent baseline certification
    2. Target roles: Security Analyst, Junior Consultant, SOC Analyst
    3. Build lab experience (home lab, HackTheBox, TryHackMe)
    4. Consider contractor roles for experience diversity
    5. Focus on cloud skills (AWS, Azure) early

    Best Markets: Austin, Dallas, Phoenix, Raleigh (value)

    Mid-Level (3-7 years)¶

    Target Salary Range: $110K - $160K

    Recommendations:

    1. Get CISSP or specialized cert (OSCP, CCSP)
    2. Specialize: Choose pen testing, cloud, GRC, or architecture
    3. Remote work widely available - negotiate hard
    4. Consider consulting firms for accelerated growth
    5. Build GitHub portfolio, blog, speaking opportunities

    Best Markets: Seattle, Boston, Austin, DC (opportunities + value)

    Senior-Level (7-15 years)¶

    Target Salary Range: $160K - $280K

    Recommendations:

    1. Multiple certifications: CISSP + specialty
    2. Management track or deep technical specialization
    3. Consider vCISO/fractional work ($150-$500/hr)
    4. Negotiate equity if startup/scale-up
    5. Build personal brand for consulting pipeline

    Best Markets: San Francisco, New York, Seattle, DC (compensation)

    Executive-Level (15+ years)¶

    Target Salary Range: $250K - $600K+

    Recommendations:

    1. CISO or vCISO (fractional for multiple clients)
    2. Board experience, public speaking critical
    3. Equity/LTI should be 20-40% of total comp
    4. Consider portfolio career (FTE + advisory boards)
    5. Industry specialization valuable (finance, healthcare, etc.)

    Best Markets: San Francisco, New York (Fortune 500), or remote multi-client vCISO

    For Employers¶

    Budget Planning¶

    Fully Loaded Cost Multipliers:

    • Entry-Level Security: 1.4x - 1.6x base salary
    • Mid-Level Security: 1.5x - 1.8x base salary
    • Senior-Level Security: 1.6x - 2.0x base salary
    • Executive-Level: 1.8x - 2.5x base salary

    Contractor Rates:

    • Budget 1.5x - 2.0x W-2 equivalent for 1099
    • Use contractors for <18 month projects
    • Full-time for strategic, long-term roles

    Competitive Compensation Strategy¶

    To Compete for Talent:

    1. Match market rate minimum (use this report)
    2. Offer remote flexibility (no salary penalty)
    3. Provide training budget ($5K-$10K/year)
    4. Certification reimbursement (100%)
    5. Conference attendance (1-2 per year)
    6. Equity for startups (0.5%-2% range)
    7. Clear career progression (6-12 month reviews)

    Alternative Strategies¶

    If Budget-Constrained:

    1. vCISO instead of full-time CISO (60-75% savings)
    2. Fractional specialists (security architect, pen tester)
    3. Hire entry-level + strong mentorship program
    4. Managed Security Services (MSSP) for commodity work
    5. Remote hiring from lower-cost markets (maintain comp, gain talent)

    For Consultants & Freelancers¶

    Rate Setting¶

    Hourly Rate Formula:

    1. Determine desired annual income (e.g., $150K)
    2. Add 30-50% for benefits/taxes ($195K-$225K)
    3. Divide by billable hours (1,200-1,800/year)
    4. Result: $108-$188/hour

    Market Check:

    • Entry Consultant: $75-$150/hr
    • Mid-Level: $150-$250/hr
    • Senior: $250-$450/hr
    • Expert: $450-$800/hr

    Specialization Premium: +20-40% for niche skills

    Business Development¶

    Building Consulting Practice:

    1. Start part-time (nights/weekends while FTE)
    2. First clients: Referrals from network
    3. Productize services: Security assessment packages
    4. Recurring revenue: Retainer clients (vCISO, advisory)
    5. Authority building: Blog, LinkedIn, speaking
    6. Niche down: Be known for ONE thing

    Revenue Targets:

    • Solo: $150K-$300K/year
    • With team: $500K-$2M+/year
    • Productized: $1M+ (courses, tools, SaaS)
