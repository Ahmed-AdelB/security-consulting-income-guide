    Security Training Platform Income Guide 2025¶

    Last Updated: December 24, 2025 Status: DRAFT Research Sources: 2024-2025 Market Data, Reddit Discussions, Salary Aggregates

    Executive Summary: The State of Security Training Income¶

    In 2025, the "passive income" dream of recording a single course and retiring is largely dead for the average instructor. The market has bifurcated into two distinct tiers: Commoditized Volume (Udemy, YouTube) and Premium Specialization (SANS, OffSec, Specialized Bootcamps).

    While entry-level generalist content faces extreme price pressure, demand for highly specialized, advanced security training (Cloud Security, AI Red Teaming, ICS/SCADA) allows top instructors to command significant premiums.

    1. The "Ivy League": High-Barrier, High-Reward¶

    SANS Institute¶

    • Model: Elite, invite-only/audition-based. Instructors are typically top-tier industry practitioners.
    • Income Potential: EXTREMELY HIGH
      • Per Event: Anecdotal reports consistently place pay between $9,000 - $13,000 per teaching week (varies by class size and seniority).
      • Annualized: A "Lead" instructor teaching 6-8 times a year can easily net $60k - $100k+ as a side income on top of their day job.
      • Full-Time Fellows: Top-tier SANS Fellows who author courseware can earn significantly more (revenue share models), potentially exceeding $300k-$500k/year from training alone.
    • Barrier to Entry: Extremely High. Requires "Mentor" program graduation, peer review, and distinct public speaking ability.

    Offensive Security (OffSec)¶

    • Model: Employee-based (mostly). Instructors and content developers are typically full-time employees.
    • Income Potential: HIGH (Salary-based)
      • Roles: "Offensive Security Engineer", "Content Developer", "Student Mentor".
      • Salary Range: $130,000 - $170,000 base salary for content creators/engineers.
      • Top Tier: Senior researchers/trainers can command $200k+ total compensation.
    • Notes: Less "passive income" potential, but highly stable with benefits. Excellent for brand building.

    2. The "Marketplace": Volume & Volatility¶

    Udemy¶

    • Model: Open marketplace. You keep a % of sales.
    • 2025 Reality: DECLINING MARGINS
      • Revenue Share: Drastic cuts in 2024/2025 for "Udemy Business" (subscription) revenue share (down to ~17.5-20%).
      • Income Potential:
        • Bottom 75%: <$1,000/year (Hobbyist).
        • Mid-Tier: $2,000 - $10,000/year (Successful side hustle).
        • Top 1%: $50,000+ (Full-time income). Requires massive volume (tens of thousands of students) or a specific niche monopoly.
      • Strategy: Success now requires bringing your own audience. Relying on organic Udemy traffic is increasingly difficult due to algorithm changes and "Udemy Business" dominance.

    Pluralsight¶

    • Model: Royalty-based (pay-per-view/minute watched). Invitation/Audition required.
    • Status: MATURE / SATURATED
      • Income: Historically, top authors made $100k - $500k/year.
      • Current Trend: "Black box" algorithms determine royalty rates. As the library grows, individual course views dilute.
      • Expectation: Good for consistent "long tail" income ($5k-$20k/year for a solid library of courses), but difficult to break into the "wealthy author" tier starting in 2025 without covering a brand-new, hot technology (e.g., specific AI Security frameworks).

    3. Independent & Creator Economy¶

    Self-Hosted (Teachable, Kajabi, Maven)¶

    • Model: You own the platform and the customer email list.
    • Income Potential: UNCAPPED but HARD
      • Metrics: A cohort-based course (CBC) charged at $500-$1,000/student with 20 students = $10k-$20k per weekend.
      • Requirement: Requires a massive personal brand (LinkedIn/Twitter/YouTube following).
      • Success Story: Specialized "Bug Bounty" or "Smart Contract Auditing" bootcamps often outperform general platforms because they sell a specific outcome (getting a job/bounty) rather than just knowledge.

    Tier Profile Platform Mix Estimated Annual Income (Training Only)
