    SECURITY MSP & MSSP INCOME GUIDE 2025¶

    Comprehensive Research on Rates, Pricing Models, and Recurring Revenue¶

    Date: December 24, 2025 Scope: Managed Security Services, SOC-as-a-Service, vCISO, MDR, and Recurring Revenue Models.

    1. Executive Summary: The 2025 Security Service Market¶

    In 2025, the Managed Security Service Provider (MSSP) market is defined by a shift from "reactive monitoring" to "proactive resilience." Clients are no longer just buying firewalls; they are buying outcomes (compliance, reduced risk, business continuity).

    Key Market Drivers: * Compliance as a Wedge: New regulations (SEC rules, CMMC 2.0, updated NIST frameworks) are forcing SMBs to seek vCISO and GRC support. * Consolidation of Tools: Buyers prefer "Platform" approaches over point solutions. MSSPs bundling SOC+MDR+GRC are winning. * Subscription Economy: 90%+ of high-performing MSSP revenue is now recurring (MRR), driven by per-user or per-asset subscriptions.

    2. MSSP Pricing Models & Rates (2025 Benchmarks)¶

    Pricing has stabilized into distinct bands based on client maturity and service depth.

    A. General Pricing Models¶

    Model Typical Range (2025) Best For Pros/Cons
    Per-User $100 - $400 / month (Total IT + Security) SMBs, Knowledge Workers Pros: Predictable, easy to sell.
    Cons: Hard to maintain margin if user has many devices.
    Per-Device/Endpoint $45 - $150 / month Asset-heavy industries (Mfg, Retail) Pros: Scales with infrastructure.
    Cons: Can discourage clients from securing all assets.
    Tiered Bundles See below Most MSSPs Pros: Simplifies choice, encourages up-sell.
    Fixed Fee / Retainer $5,000 - $20,000 / month Mid-Market / Enterprise Pros: Guaranteed revenue.
    Cons: Scope creep risk.

    B. Tiered Service Bundles (The "Good-Better-Best" Strategy)¶

    Successful MSSPs in 2025 typically offer three distinct tiers.

    Tier 1: Foundational Security (The "Must Haves")¶

    • Target: Small Business / Low Compliance needs.
    • Price Point: $45 - $60 per endpoint/month (or ~$120/user/month bundled with IT).
    • Inclusions:
      • Managed EDR (Endpoint Detection & Response).
      • Patch Management.
      • Basic Email Security (Spam/Phishing).
      • Web/DNS Filtering.
      • Automated Weekly Reporting.
    • Margin Goal: 60%+ (High automation).

    Tier 2: Enhanced Security (The "Sleep at Night")¶

    • Target: Growth SMBs, regulated industries (Finance/Healthcare Lite).
    • Price Point: $75 - $100 per endpoint/month (or ~$180-$220/user/month).
    • Inclusions (Adds to Tier 1):
      • 24/7 SOC Monitoring (Eyes on glass).
      • Managed Detection & Response (MDR).
      • Vulnerability Management (Monthly scans + remediation guidance).
      • SaaS/Cloud Security (M365 Monitoring).
      • Security Awareness Training.
    • Margin Goal: 50% (Requires SOC labor/vendor costs).

    Tier 3: Premium / Compliance (The "Audit Ready")¶

    • Target: Mid-Market, Highly Regulated (CMMC, HIPAA, FINRA).
    • Price Point: $130 - $200+ per endpoint/month (or flat $5k - $20k/mo).
    • Inclusions (Adds to Tier 2):
      • vCISO Services (Quarterly strategy, policy review).
      • SIEM Log Ingestion & Retention (1 year+).
      • Advanced Threat Hunting.
      • Incident Response Retainer (Priority access).
      • Third-Party Risk Management.
    • Margin Goal: 40-50% (High labor, high value).

    3. SOC as a Service (SOCaaS) Pricing¶

    For MSPs looking to resell, or MSSPs building their stack.

    Wholesale / Reseller Costs (Cost to MSP)¶

    • Per Endpoint Model: $8 - $15 per endpoint/month.
      • Includes: 24/7 automated monitoring + human triage of critical alerts.
    • Per User Model: $10 - $25 per user/month.
      • Includes: Endpoint + M365/Email monitoring.
    • Flat Rate SOC: $1,500 - $3,000 / month block for up to X endpoints.

    Retail Pricing (Price to End Client)¶

    • Markup: Typically 2x - 3x wholesale cost.
    • Standalone SOCaaS: Sold at $30 - $60 per endpoint/month to clients who already have IT but need security.
    • Minimums: Many providers enforce a $1,500/month minimum to engage.

    Build vs. Buy (2025 Reality)¶

    • Building In-House SOC: Requires minimum 6-8 analysts for true 24/7 coverage.
      • Est. Cost: $800k - $1.2M / year (Salaries, Tools, Training).
    • Outsourcing SOCaaS:
      • Est. Cost: $50k - $100k / year for a mid-sized MSP to cover their client base.
      • Verdict: Outsourcing is the only viable path for 95% of MSPs.

    4. vCISO & Strategic Services Rates¶

    The highest margin, highest "stickiness" service in 2025.

    Hourly Rates¶

    • General vCISO: $200 - $350 / hour.
    • Specialist (CMMC, FedRAMP, Forensic): $350 - $600 / hour.
    • Note: Hourly is discouraged in favor of retainers to ensure recurring revenue.

    Retainer Models (Recurring Revenue)¶

    • Level 1: Advisory (Virtual Board Member)
      • Deliverables: Monthly meeting, Quarterly strategy review, basic policy maintenance.
      • Rate: $2,000 - $4,000 / month.
    • Level 2: Program Management (Compliance Lead)
      • Deliverables: Managing an active compliance program (e.g., SOC 2 readiness), vendor assessments, staff training oversight.
      • Rate: $4,500 - $8,000 / month.
    • Level 3: Full Outsourced CISO (Enterprise Lite)
      • Deliverables: Weekly engagement, board reporting, full security team leadership, incident command.
      • Rate: $8,500 - $15,000+ / month.

    Project Fees (One-Offs)¶

    • Gap Assessment / Risk Analysis: $5,000 - $15,000.
    • Incident Response (Non-Retainer): $350/hr with $5,000 retainer upfront.
    • Policy Development Pack: $3,000 - $8,000.

    5. Managed SIEM & MDR Specifics¶

    Managed SIEM (Log Aggregation & Compliance)¶

    • Pricing Driver: Data Volume (GB/day) or EPS (Events Per Second).
    • Small Org (<50GB/day): $1,500 - $4,000 / month.
    • Asset-Based Alternative: $15 - $25 per asset / month (Includes ingestion & retention).
    • Compliance Surcharge: +20% for "Cold Storage" requirements (e.g., 7-year retention).

    Managed Detection & Response (MDR)¶

    • Per Endpoint: $10 - $30 / month (Wholesale/Cost).
    • Retail Price: $25 - $75 / month.
    • Volume Pricing (e.g., SentinelOne Vigilance, CrowdStrike Falcon Complete):
      • 100 Endpoints: ~$50k/year.
      • 10,000 Endpoints: ~$500k/year (Significant economies of scale).

    6. Recurring Revenue (MRR) Strategy for 2025¶

    To build a "Massive" income stream, successful firms are moving away from "Line Items" and towards "Outcome Bundles."

    The "Security Stack" MRR Calculation¶

    Example for a 50-user Client:

    1. Foundational Stack (User Support): $150/user $\times$ 50 = $7,500/mo
    2. Advanced Security Layer (MDR/SOC): $50/user $\times$ 50 = $2,500/mo
    3. vCISO Retainer (Compliance/Strategy): Flat Fee = $3,000/mo
    4. Cloud Backup/DR: $20/user $\times$ 50 = $1,000/mo

    Total MRR: $14,000 / month ($168k/year) from one client.

    Key Takeaway¶

    The money is in the convergence. Do not sell "Antivirus" ($3 profit). Sell "Ransomware Protection & Warranty" ($50 profit). Do not sell "Consulting Hours" ($200 once). Sell "Compliance Assurance" ($4,000/month forever).
