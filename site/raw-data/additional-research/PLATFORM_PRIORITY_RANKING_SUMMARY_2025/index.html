    Bug Bounty Platform Priority Rankings - Quick Reference Guide 2025¶

    Last Updated: December 24, 2025

    TOP 5 PLATFORMS FOR MAXIMUM EARNINGS¶

    1. IMMUNEFI - Web3/DeFi King¶

    • Max Bounty: $14.82M (record), $10M (Wormhole)
    • Total Paid: $116M+
    • Top Earner: LonelySloth ($3.6M)
    • 30 millionaires from platform
    • Best for: Smart contract auditors, Solidity experts
    • Entry barrier: HIGH (requires Solidity/Vyper expertise)

    2. SPEARBIT - Ultimate Elite¶

    • Max Earnings: $200k-1M/year
    • Weekly Pay: $3k-20k USDC (JSR to LSR progression)
    • Revenue Split: 70-80% to auditors (vs 30% at firms)
    • Best for: Industry veterans, senior auditors
    • Entry barrier: EXTREME (industry veteran status required)

    3. HACKERONE - Industry Standard¶

    • Platform Payout: $81M/year
    • Top Earners: $300k+/year
    • Largest community: 1.5M+ researchers
    • Programs: 1,950+ (most variety)
    • Best for: All levels, especially beginners
    • Entry barrier: LOW

    4. SYNACK RED TEAM - Elite Efficiency¶

    • Unique Feature: Still paid for duplicates
    • Payment Speed: Same day (fastest)
    • Earnings: Microsoft-level salaries
    • Acceptance Rate: 20% of applicants
    • Best for: Experienced hunters, part-time work
    • Entry barrier: HIGH (rigorous vetting)

    5. CANTINA - Elite Competitions¶

    • Largest Competition: $2.35M (Uniswap v4)
    • Total Paid: $16.1M+
    • Fellowship: Top 5% earn $10k+/contest
    • TVL Secured: $25B+
    • Best for: Elite auditors, competitive spirits
    • Entry barrier: HIGH

    PLATFORM DECISION MATRIX¶

    If You're a BEGINNER (0-1 year)¶

    Start with: HackerOne or Bugcrowd

    Priority Order:

    1. HackerOne - Hacker101 training, 1.5M community
    2. Bugcrowd - Best triage (99% SLA), faster payments
    3. Intigriti - European focus, easy setup

    Expected Timeline:

    • 6-12 months to first payout
    • $500-2,000/month if consistent (15-20hrs/week)
    • 50-70% duplicate rate initially

    DON'T START WITH:

    • Synack (impossible without experience)
    • Immunefi (need Solidity first)
    • Spearbit (veterans only)

    If You're INTERMEDIATE (1-3 years)¶

    Priority Order:

    1. HackerOne Private Programs - 2-5x higher payouts
    2. Bugcrowd Private Programs - Better experience
    3. Intigriti - European programs
    4. YesWeHack - GDPR compliance
    5. Code4rena - IF learning smart contracts

    Expected Earnings: $2,000-5,000/month

    Goal: Build >50% acceptance rate, get private invites

    Next Steps:

    • Specialize (API, mobile, logic flaws)
    • OR learn Solidity for DeFi pivot
    • Start building public reputation

    If You're ADVANCED (3-5 years)¶

    Priority Order:

    1. Immunefi - $10M+ bounties (if Solidity skills)
    2. Synack - Apply NOW (20% acceptance)
    3. Sherlock - LSW $10k/week opportunities
    4. Cantina - Fellowship program (top 5%)
    5. HackerOne - Top programs (Google, Meta, Crypto.com)

    Expected Earnings: $100,000-300,000+/year

    Career Decisions:

    • Full-time bug bounty
    • Security firm
    • Smart contract auditor
    • Security consultant

    If You're ELITE/CAREER (5+ years)¶

    Ultimate Goals (in order):

    1. Spearbit - $200k-1M/year, 70-80% revenue retention
    2. Synack Red Team - Same-day pay, no duplicate stress
    3. Immunefi All Stars - $10M bounties, 30 millionaires
    4. Sherlock LSW - $10k/week + pool share
    5. Cantina Fellow - $10k+/contest bonus

    Diversification Strategy:

    • Continuous income (Synack) + high bounties (Immunefi)
    • Multiple platforms simultaneously
    • Own consulting/auditing firm
    • Training/education content
    • Conference circuit

    Beginner (0-1 year)¶

    • Monthly: $500-2,000 (15-20hrs/week)
    • Annual: $6,000-24,000
    • First payout: 6-12 months
    • Reality: 50-70% duplicates initially

    Intermediate (1-3 years)¶

    • Monthly: $2,000-5,000
    • Annual: $24,000-60,000
    • Private invites: Starting to receive
    • Specialization: Developing

    Advanced (3-5 years)¶

    • Monthly: $5,000-25,000
    • Annual: $60,000-300,000
    • Elite access: Synack, Immunefi
    • Career decision: Full-time or supplement

    Elite (5+ years)¶

    • Annual: $100,000-300,000+
    • Top platforms: Spearbit, Synack, Immunefi
    • Diversification: Multiple income streams
    • Reputation: Industry recognized

    CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS¶

    Top 5% Success Factors¶

    1. >50% acceptance rate on submissions
    2. Specialize in high-value vulnerabilities (RCE, auth bypass, logic flaws)
    3. Target private programs (2-5x higher pay)
    4. Use AI tools (70% of top hunters do)
    5. Network actively with community

    Why 95% Quit¶

    1. Expect immediate money (unrealistic)
    2. Give up after duplicates
    3. No specialization
    4. Chase every platform (no focus)
    5. Don't network or learn

    RED FLAGS - AVOID THESE¶

    Platform Red Flags:

    • Programs with <10 resolved reports
    • No public payouts visible
    • Threatening legal language
    • Unresponsive (30+ days no triage)
    • Change scope after report
    • Only offer "swag" or "recognition"

    HackerOne Specific:

    • Payment delays (weeks to months)
    • Mediation ineffective (criticized)
    • Platform sides with customers
    • Some programs "ghost" researchers

    THE REALITY CHECK¶

    95% of bug bounty hunters quit. Only 5% make consistent money. The top 5% earn 50% of all bounties paid.

    Timeline to success:

    • 6-12 months to first $1,000 month
    • 2-3 years to sustainable full-time income
    • 5+ years to elite status

    This is NOT:

    • Get rich quick
    • Easy passive income
    • Guaranteed money

    This IS:

    • Highly competitive
    • Skill-intensive
    • Requires persistence
    • Intellectually rewarding
    • Can be lucrative for top 5%
