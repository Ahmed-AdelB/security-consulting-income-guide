    Platform Deep Dives¶

    Comprehensive guides for every platform where cybersecurity professionals can earn consulting income.

    Platform Categories¶

    • Expert Networks

    Connect with investors and executives for high-paying consultation calls. - GLG, AlphaSights, Guidepoint - $500-$2,000/hour rates - Flexible, on-demand work

    Expert Networks

    • Bug Bounty Platforms

    Find vulnerabilities and get paid by major companies. - HackerOne, Bugcrowd, Synack - $100-$50,000+ per bug - Build reputation and portfolio

    Bug Bounty

    • vCISO Platforms

    Provide fractional CISO services to multiple clients. - SideChannel, FRSecure, RealCISO - $5,000-$15,000/month retainers - Recurring revenue model

    vCISO

    • Expert Witness

    Provide litigation support and testimony for legal cases. - SEAK, Expert Institute, Round Table - $300-$1,000+/hour rates - High-stakes, high-pay work

    Expert Witness

    • Web3 Security Audits

    Audit smart contracts and blockchain protocols. - Code4rena, Immunefi, Spearbit - $30,000-$500,000+ per contest - Cutting-edge technology

    Web3 Audit

    Platform Comparison¶

    Platform Type Rate Range Time Commitment Difficulty Best For
    Expert Networks $500-$2,000/hr 1-2 hrs/call Low All levels
    Bug Bounty $100-$50K/bug Flexible High Technical hunters
    vCISO $5K-$15K/mo 10-40 hrs/mo Medium Senior security
    Expert Witness $300-$1,000/hr Variable Medium Domain experts
    Web3 Audit $30K-$500K/contest 1-4 weeks Very High Smart contract experts

    Getting Started¶

    1. Choose your focus: Pick 1-2 platform types that match your skills
    2. Read the deep dives: Each section has detailed platform breakdowns
    3. Start with highest ROI: Expert networks often have the best effort-to-income ratio
    4. Build your profile: Create compelling profiles on each platform
    5. Track and optimize: Monitor which platforms generate the most income
