    Security Training Content Creator Guide 2025¶

    Last Updated: December 24, 2025 Status: Draft Research Inputs: TRAINING_PLATFORM_INCOME_GUIDE_2025.md, CYBERSECURITY_TRAINING_SPEAKING_INCOME_COMPREHENSIVE_GUIDE_2025.md, TRAINING_SPEAKING_INCOME_GUIDE_2025.md

    Executive Summary¶

    • Courses out-earn books for recurring income. Royalties from Udemy and Pluralsight scale with catalog depth, while technical book royalties are modest and front-loaded.
    • Udemy is volume-driven. Most instructors earn under $1,000/year, but top cybersecurity instructors can reach $50K-$200K+/year with strong marketing and large catalogs.
    • Pluralsight is a long-tail royalty model. Established authors (5+ courses) can reach $40K-$100K/year; top 1% can exceed $400K/year.
    • Security book advances are small. Advances cluster around $5K-$10K with lifetime earnings often $5K-$30K; best-sellers are rare outliers.
    • Passive income is real but not instant. Expect 100-200 hours per Pluralsight course and 300-1,000 hours per book before meaningful revenue.

    Passive Income Potential Snapshot (Annualized, $K)¶

    Channel Passive Income Potential Core Data Points
    Udemy $0K-$200K+/year Average $3.3K/year; 75% under $1K; top 1% $50K+; top cybersecurity instructors $50K-$200K+
    Pluralsight $5K-$400K+/year Year 1 $5K-$15K; established $40K-$100K; top 1% $400K+; platform average ~$40K
    Security Books $0K-$10K/year typical Advances $5K-$10K; lifetime $5K-$30K per book; best-seller $50K-$100K+ (rare)

    Interpretation: Udemy and Pluralsight can become meaningful passive income only with multiple courses, while books are more valuable for authority and lead generation than cashflow.

    Udemy Instructor Earnings Research¶

    Compensation Model

    • Instructor promotions: 97% revenue share
    • Udemy promotions: 37% revenue share
    • Marketplace pricing is aggressive and discount-driven

    Earnings Distribution (Internal Research)

    • Average instructor: ~$3,306/year
    • 75% of instructors: <$1,000/year
    • Top 1%: $50,000+/year
    • Top 10 earners: $500,000-$8,000,000 lifetime

    Realistic Income Scenarios

    Scenario Annual Range Typical Inputs
    First course, minimal marketing $0.5K-$2K 40-100 hours build, light promotion
    3-5 courses with marketing $5K-$15K Consistent release + audience building
    10+ course catalog $20K-$50K High volume, aggressive updates
    Top cybersecurity instructor $50K-$200K+ Strong brand, certifications, niche control

    Passive Income Reality

    • Most earnings come from self-promotion, not marketplace traffic.
    • Udemy Business revenue share cuts reduce margins for subscription traffic.
    • Long-tail income exists, but only for courses kept updated and heavily marketed.

    Pluralsight Author Royalties Research¶

    Compensation Model

    • Royalty-based on minutes viewed (share of total platform consumption).
    • Platform discovery handles most marketing, but catalog depth matters.

    Earnings Benchmarks

    • Top 5 instructors: ~$400K/year
    • Platform average: ~$40K/year for active instructors
    • New author year 1: $5K-$15K
    • Year 2-3 (2-3 courses): $15K-$40K
    • Established (5+ courses): $40K-$100K/year
    • Top 1%: $400K+/year

    Production Economics

    • 10-20 hours of work per 1 hour of finished content
    • 100-200+ hours per course (scripting, recording, editing)
    • Ongoing updates required to stay visible in the library

    Passive Income Reality

    • Royalties are long-tail; income compounds as the catalog grows.
    • New authors should expect low, volatile earnings until 2-3 courses are live.

    Security Book Advances & Royalties Research¶

    Compensation Model

    • Advance: $5,000-$10,000 (some reports under $5K for O'Reilly)
    • Royalties: 10-18% typical, 5-35% range depending on publisher
    • Average lifetime earnings: $5,000-$10,000 per book

    Income Scenarios

    • Average technical book: $5K-$10K lifetime
    • Successful book: $15K-$30K lifetime
    • Best-seller (rare): $50K-$100K+ lifetime
    • Ongoing royalties: Often minimal after launch peak

    Time Investment

    • Writing: 300-1,000 hours
    • Reviews/edits: 50-100 hours
    • Marketing: ongoing, mostly self-driven

    Passive Income Reality

    • Book income is usually a one-time advance + short sales curve.
    • The real upside is authority that boosts consulting, training, and expert witness opportunities.

    2025 Content Strategy for Security Creators¶

    High-Leverage Niches (From Platform Research)

    1. AI Red Teaming / LLM Security
    2. Cloud Native Security (Kubernetes, Serverless)
    3. Smart Contract Auditing (Solidity/Rust)
    4. ICS/OT Security

    Content Positioning Principles

    • Build sequences: intro → intermediate → advanced for compounding royalties.
    • Anchor each course to job outcomes (cert prep, role readiness, or hands-on labs).
    • Avoid saturated “Intro to Cybersecurity” topics unless you own the audience.

    Course + Book Portfolio Architecture¶

    Goal: Balance marketplace discovery with royalty stability and authority.

    Asset Purpose Best Use
    Udemy course High-volume discovery Entry-level reach, email list capture
    Pluralsight course Long-tail royalties Evergreen, enterprise-grade topics
    Technical book Authority + credibility Consulting, speaking, premium training

    Stacking Strategy

    1. Launch 1 Udemy course to validate topic demand.
    2. Repurpose into a Pluralsight course for royalty longevity.
    3. Publish a technical book to cement authority and open premium doors.

    12-Month Execution Plan (Security Creator)¶

    Months 1-3: Validation + First Course

    • Pick one high-demand niche and draft a 6-8 hour syllabus.
    • Build a Udemy course (40-100 production hours).
    • Capture early feedback from 50-100 students.

    Months 4-6: Catalog Build

    • Ship course #2 (advanced module or labs).
    • Apply to Pluralsight with a focused proposal.
    • Start a mailing list and LinkedIn content cadence.

    Months 7-9: Royalty Expansion

    • Launch first Pluralsight course.
    • Refresh Udemy course content and add practice labs.
    • Identify a book outline that maps to the course series.

    Months 10-12: Authority Asset

    • Submit a book proposal (advance target $5K-$10K).
    • Prepare a premium workshop version for corporate clients.
    • Consolidate case studies and learner outcomes.

    Key Risks & Mitigation¶

    Risk Impact Mitigation
    Marketplace saturation Price pressure, low royalties Focus on niche depth and outcomes
    Royalty volatility Unpredictable cashflow Diversify across Udemy + Pluralsight
    Book ROI is low High time cost Use books as authority, not income
    Content decay Earnings drop Update courses every 12-18 months

    Bottom Line¶

    Security content creation can deliver $5K-$100K+ in annual passive income once a course catalog is established, with Pluralsight providing the most reliable long-tail royalties and Udemy delivering the broadest reach. Technical books rarely create large direct income, but they remain a powerful credential that unlocks higher consulting and training rates.
