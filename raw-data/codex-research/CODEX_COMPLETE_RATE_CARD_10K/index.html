    2.1 Virtual CISO (vCISO) & Advisory¶

    The highest margin consulting role for experienced leaders.

    Service Tier Rate / Price Model Typical Scope
    Hourly Advisory $200 - $500 / hr Ad-hoc strategy, Board meeting prep, Vendor selection.
    vCISO Lite $1,500 - $5,000 / mo 5-10 hrs/mo. Policy review, Quarterly risk check.
    vCISO Standard $5,000 - $12,000 / mo 10-25 hrs/mo. Compliance readiness, Vendor mgmt, Monthly reporting.
    vCISO Enterprise $12,000 - $25,000 / mo 25-50 hrs/mo. Program build, Audit defense, Staff oversight.
    Gap Assessment $15,000 - $40,000 Fixed fee. Full roadmap (NIST/ISO/SOC2) + Risk Register.

    2.2 Offensive Security (Pentest / Red Team)¶

    Rates vary heavily by target complexity and "Rules of Engagement" (ROE).

    Service Type Rate Range Notes
    Network Pentest $150 - $300 / hr Internal/External infrastructure. Standard methodology.
    Web App Pentest $200 - $400 / hr OWASP Top 10 focus. Pricing often per-endpoint or per-role.
    Mobile App (iOS/Android) $250 - $450 / hr Requires binary analysis, API testing, and specialized tooling.
    Red Team (Objective) $30,000 - $150,000+ Fixed fee per 4-12 week campaign. Physical + Digital + Social.

    8. üìù Contract & Payment Terms (Best Practices)¶

    1. Retainers: Always bill prepaid. Do not start work until the month's fee is in the bank.
    2. Payment Terms: Standard is Net 15 or Net 30. Avoid "Net 60" or "Pay when Paid" clauses.
    3. Late Fees: Include 1.5% interest per month on overdue invoices.
    4. Rush Fees: Add 25% - 50% surcharge for work required within <48 hours or weekends.
    5. Kill Fee: If a project is cancelled after scheduling, charge 50% of the remaining fee (or 100% if <48 hours notice).
    6. Travel: Client pays all hard costs (Airfare, Hotel, Uber) + 50% of hourly rate for travel time.
